WHO WE ARE

Blockchain@CSUN is a community of students, faculty, staff, and alumni focused on advancing discussion and development of blockchain and digital assets. Our mission is to provide education while also learning together about this profoundly transformative technology.

We share an interest in exploring the relationship between distributed systems and human incentives, and its impact on traditional fields including finance, computer science, and economics. All disciplines welcome!
